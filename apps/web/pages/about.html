About ClearPilot

ClearPilot is a two-part study companion for SAP CPI (Cloud Integration) job seekers, built by someone going through that exact job search. The web app is where you study; the desktop app is where you perform.

The web app

Ask questions about adapters, mappings, security artifacts, and real integration scenarios. Answers are grounded in official SAP Integration Suite documentation and SAP Learning courses, plus whatever resume, job description, and notes you bring to your account.

The desktop app

ClearPilot Desktop runs invisibly during a real interview — listening to the conversation, matching questions against your prep, and surfacing an answer on your screen in milliseconds. It stays out of the taskbar, Alt-Tab, and screen-share capture.

One account

Sign in once. Materials, training data, and history you build on the web are available on desktop, and the reverse — see Data & Privacy for exactly what's shared and how.
